Zenless Zone Zero

Zenless Zone Zero is an action-packed game set in a futuristic city where reality intersects with mysterious dimensions
called Hollows. Players take on the role of a Proxy, guiding various characters through dynamic, fast-paced battles
against powerful monsters.

The game features a stylish cyberpunk aesthetic with fluid combat mechanics, allowing seamless character switching and
combo attacks. Each character has unique abilities and fighting styles, encouraging strategic team compositions.
